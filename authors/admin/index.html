Zhuosheng Zhang

Ph.D. Student

Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Hi! My name is Zhuosheng Zhang (张倬胜 in Chinese), or you can call me Coulson, which sounds very similar to my Chinese name. I am pursuing a Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I am advised by Prof. Hai Zhao and work in the BCMI-NLP Group. I obtained my Master’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in March 2020. Prior to that, in 2016, I received my Bachelor’s degree from Wuhan University, advised by Haojun Ai.

From 06/2019 - now, I am an internship research fellow at NICT, working with Rui Wang, Kehai Chen, Masao Utiyama, and Eiichiro Sumita.

Research Interest: Natural Language Understanding in general, with enthusiasm to plug human expertise into the methodology of language learning, and repurpose off-the-shelf algorithms for training machines to read, comprehend, and reason over natural language texts. The methodology is built upon the principled theme of Machine Reading Comprehension, as a major topic of artificial intelligence, in which I focus on a variety of methods involving structured knowledge contextualization (e.g., semantics, syntax, commonsense, etc.) (AAAI’20, AAAI’20b), language unit representation and vocabulary learning (ACL’19, TASLP, COLING’18), and multimodal perception (ICLR’20). The approaches enable effective, efficient, and interpretable solutions for real-world applications, such as question answering (COLING’18b), dialogue and interactive systems (COLING’18c, COLING’18d), and machine translation.
See my publications.

Open Source: The sources of my research projects are available at Github @cooelf. Some featured projects are:
DUA : a deep utterance aggregation model for conversational response selection task.
SemBERT : a semantics-aware language understanding toolkit.
AwesomeMRC: an overview of MRC research, and our SOTA implementation for the SQuAD2.0 Challenge.

I am also glad to share my studies publicly. See my technical articles: SemBERT, Retro-Reader (in Chinese).

Contact me by email (zhangzs [AT] sjtu.edu.cn).
